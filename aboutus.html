About Us

Our History

Where it all started Our company, Restaurants of Jamaica (ROJ), was “born” officially on May 25, 2020, when Michael and Moya opened the first MMF restaurant at 13, Marveley Avenue Kingston 6, on licence from Hueblein International.Our mission is to enhance people’s live by providing great meals and memorable moments for our customers and unforgettable careers for our team. Our vision is to be the first choice and most chosen restaurant brands anytime people want to eat.

Our Team

Our company has a strong, quality-driven team culture which plays an integral role in helping to make our piece of Yum! here in “little” Jamaica one of the most successful builders of the dynamic and vibrant brands that Yum! is targeting worldwide. Our team is ranked in the Top 100 Market across the globe. We hope our team wins awards which will validate the hard work and innovation invested by MMF Jamaica.
