FAQs

How can I provide feedback for an experience I had in a restaurant?

If you have either a good or bad experience at our restaurants, we recommend that you speak with the restaurant owner or manager right away. Our Restaurant Owners would love to hear from you. Check out below to see if your question is there, or follow up with our Guest Feedback team!

What do I do if I receive a wrong order?

Our restaurant owners are committed to your satisfaction. If there is ever a time where you’re not happy with your order or have received the incorrect product(s), please do not hesitate to speak with the restaurant manager on your next visit so they can make it right for you.

I faced an app error while building my order what do I do?

We're sorry to hear you saw an issue - our team is constantly working to improve every aspect of our App and Website. You will receive an email to confirm your order and if you don't receive an email your order was not sent to the restaurant and you will not be charged. You can then re-build your order and enjoy!

How do I sign in if I already have an account?

When you go to the 'Sign Up' page at the top there is an option to 'Sign In.' This will send a link to the email account you registered with which will sign you in to your account

I can't add a new card at checkout (multiple errors)

A small portion of Guests are experiencing issues at checkout. If you experience any challenges while added a new card at checkout you can check our Contact Us page

Where can I find information about jobs, careers and applying to MMF?

We're so excited for your interest and have a number of positions open on the team! Please visit https://company.mmfoods.com/careers/bring-it-bkc
